THE STUDIO STORY

AESTHETICS & PRECISION

Established in 1948, Modern Photo Studio has been serving families and customers in Anand for over 75 years.

Located on Anand Station Road, we are known for capturing precious moments with creativity, quality, and care.

Over the years, we have built strong relationships with our customers, many of whom have trusted us for generations. Our commitment to excellent service and customer satisfaction has made us one of the most trusted photography studios in the area.

Whether it's a wedding, engagement, birthday, baby shoot, maternity shoot, or any special occasion, our experienced team works passionately to create beautiful memories that last a lifetime.

As we continue to grow, we remain dedicated to providing professional photography services while embracing new trends and technologies to serve our customers even better.
